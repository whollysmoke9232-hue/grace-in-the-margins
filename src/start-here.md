## Start Here

If you’re not sure where to begin, these guided reading paths are offered as **companions**, not instructions.

Choose the path that best reflects where you are right now.  
There is no right order. There is no rush.

---

## If You Are Suffering or Waiting

Sometimes the ache is not only the pain itself, but the long waiting that follows.
These readings are offered as quiet company for seasons of grief, uncertainty, and endurance.

- [Be Still](/devotionals/be-still/)
- [The Gift of Now](/meditations/the-gift-of-now/)
- [If God Is Everywhere](/meditations/if-god-is-everywhere/)
- [When Jesus Wept](/devotionals/when-jesus-wept/)
- [A Meditation in Suffering](/meditations/a-meditation-in-suffering/)
- [New Every Morning](/devotionals/new-every-morning/)

---

## If You Are Wrestling With Sin, Failure, or Shame

These readings do not minimize consequences or offer quick relief.
They speak to the experience of reckoning, repentance, and the slow work of restoration—without turning away.

- [The Bitter Harvest of Sin](/testimonies/bitter-harvest-of-sin/)
- [Jesus – The Scapegoat](/testimonies/jesus-the-scapegoat/)
- [Purify My Heart](/devotionals/purify-my-heart/)
- [From Ashes to Glory](/testimonies/from-ashes-to-glory/)
- [In the Margins](/testimonies/in-the-margins/)

## If You Feel Spiritually Numb, Dry, or Disconnected

There are seasons when faith remains, but feeling does not.
These readings are meant to reawaken attention—to help you notice where nourishment may still be quietly present.

- [What Are You Feeding?](/reflections/what-are-you-feeding/)
- [Seek His Face, Not Just His Hand](/devotionals/seek-his-face/)
- [Mustard Seed Faith](/devotionals/mustard-seed-faith)
- [Look and Live](/devotionals/look-and-live/)
- [Faith, From Beginning to End](/reflections/faith-from-beginning-to-end/)

## If You Long to Know Who You Really Are

When life fractures, identity often follows.
These readings return to Scripture’s quieter affirmations—where naming, calling, and belonging are restored over time, not declared all at once.

- [What's In A Name](/reflections/whats-in-a-name/)
- [After God’s Heart](/testimonies/after-gods-heart/)
- [Held on Every Side](/reflections/held-on-every-side/)
- [Working from Grace](/reflections/working-from-grace/)

---

## If You Learn Best Through Story and Image

Some truths enter the heart more easily through story than through explanation.
These readings invite imagination and reflection, allowing meaning to surface gently rather than being argued into place.

- [I Wanna Dance Like Enoch Danced](/dance-like-enoch-danced/)
- [The House That Calls to the Simple](/stories/the-house-that-calls-to-the-simple)
